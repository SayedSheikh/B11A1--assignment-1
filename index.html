Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation leads Bangladesh's progress by creating new ideas, solutions, and opportunities for growth.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on protecting Bangladesh’s environment, resources, and future through responsible development.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure development boosts Bangladesh’s economy by improving roads, transport, energy, and communication systems.

Empowerment

Empowerment helps people in Bangladesh gain skills, confidence, and opportunities to improve their lives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

I am committed to fulfilling the dream of the students and the people with which they jumped into the movement for a state without discrimination and exploitation. I invite all of you to come forward with all your might to fulfill this dream today. Their dreams are our dreams. The youth have created a great opportunity in our national life. We urge everyone to take advantage of this opportunity.We are committed to ensuring that no form of discrimination can disrupt our dreams."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
